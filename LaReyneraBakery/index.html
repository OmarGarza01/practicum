About us

Founded in 1974 by Raul Hernandez, La reynera bakery was one of the first bakery's and tortilla factory in the Pharr Texas. Raul was inspired to open his own bakery prior to working many year at his cousins bakery. La Reynera Bakery was family own and operated and was very successful at the time. Now La Reynera Bakery is operated by three of his sons, Raul, Agustin, and David. They have continued the family tradition in offering the best barbacoa in town.

What we Offer

Here at The Reynera Bakery you will find all the items to make your own tamales. We only buy the freshest ingredients for your tamales needs. We have the best corn dough for your tortillas or tamales made fresh daily. We also have sweet Mexican bread, and we rent out chairs and tables for your special occasions. We also have the best barbacoa in town.
